Privacy Policy

Updated: November 2025

1. Controller

This app is provided by fbatra. For privacy questions, contact us at fbatra@icloud.com.

2. What we process

  • Study progress (answered questions, bookmarks, quiz results) is stored locally on your device.
  • App preferences (e.g., ad removal) are saved in UserDefaults.
  • If Google AdMob is active, pseudonymous usage data may be collected by Google; Google processes this data independently.

3. Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob. Google may collect anonymized usage data. See Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. In-app purchases

Purchases (e.g., remove ads) are processed by Apple. We do not receive personal payment data—only the entitlement status to disable ads.

5. Storage & deletion

All study data is stored locally and can be cleared anytime via Settings (“Reset progress”). Removing the app also deletes all stored data.

6. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your stored data. Contact us at fbatra@icloud.com.

7. Changes

We may update this policy as needed. The current version is always available in the app.
